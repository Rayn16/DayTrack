☀️ How DayTrack Works
✅ Tasks
Tap + New Task to create a task. Choose One-time for a single-day task, or Recurring 🔄 to repeat it on specific days of the week (or every day). Tap a task to mark it done. Tap ✕ to delete it. Use ▲▼ to reorder.
🔴 Priority
When adding a task, pick High, Medium, or Low priority. A colored dot appears on the task row so you can spot what matters most at a glance.
📋 Subtasks
Break a task into smaller steps by adding subtasks when creating it. Type a step and press Enter (or tap +). On the task list, tap the 📋 X/Y badge to expand and check off individual steps.
⏰ Reminders
Set a reminder At a time (fires once at a specific hour) or Every… 🔁 (repeats on an interval, e.g. every 2h). Reminders fire as push notifications even when the app is closed — just activate notifications in Settings first.
🔥 Streaks
Complete all of today's tasks to earn a day on your overall streak (shown at the top of the Tasks tab). Recurring tasks also show their own individual 🔥 streak badge — how many consecutive days in a row you've completed that specific task.
⏱️ Timer
Track time across multiple steps in one session. Tap + Add for each step, name it, then ▶ to start / ⏸ to pause / ✓ to lock it. Tap 💾 Save Session when done — it appears in Past Sessions and counts toward your Summary stats.
📅 Calendar
Tap any day to see a detailed breakdown: progress bar, which tasks were done vs not done, and a notes section. Colored dots on the calendar show activity: 🟢 green = all tasks done, 🟠 orange = partial, purple = tasks exist but none done.
📊 Summary
See your stats at a glance: today's completion, overall streak, total time tracked, and session count. The This Week bar chart shows daily completion % for the last 7 days. The Activity heatmap shows the past 90 days — darker green means more tasks completed.
⚙️ Settings
Push Notifications — tap Activate to enable background reminders. Dark Mode — switch themes. Export — download a JSON backup of all your tasks, sessions, and notes. Import — restore from a backup file. Every Sunday at 8pm you'll receive a weekly recap notification.
